Problem:

BCCI is planning  to stage a T20 match for collecting funds for cancer foundation society. 
The planning committee  has three grounds in consideration  for the match
                                1)MA Chidamaram Stadium , Chennai
                                2)Eden Gardens, Kolkata
                                3)Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium, Dharmasala

To decide the venue, current and EX cricket players are requested to select one of the grounds. 
Based on the data received from the cricketers we would  predict what will be their selection.



Dataset:

This data set has Player,wickets,runs and Selection.

Solution:

Classification using  Naive Bayes,SVM (RBF kernel) and Logistic  classification algorithms.

Libraries used: Numpy,Scipy,Matplotlib  and Sklearn
