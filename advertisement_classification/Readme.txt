Problem:

XYZ Motors is releasing three new upgraded cars this year - an SUV , a hatch back and a crossover
They want to send an email advertisement to its employees based on the purchase data of last year.
Last year an SUV , a hatch back and a crossover was released and the purchasing data was recorded so that 
the advertisement can be sent based on the last years purchase. This will  target potential customers , rather 
than sending incorrect ads to non interested customers.

Dataset:

This data set has Age, estimate salary and purchase columns.

Solution:

Classification using KNN,SVM with RBF kernel and logistic regression algorithms.

Libraries used: Numpy,Scipy,Matplotlib  and Sklearn
